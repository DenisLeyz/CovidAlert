Organized Social Events

  • Social gatherings:
    Indoors - 10 people
    Outdoors - 25 people
  • Staffed businesses and facilities:
    Indoors - 50 people
    Outdoors - 100 people
  • Religious services:
    Indoors - 30% capacity
    Outdoors - 100 people

Food & drink establishments

  • No buffet service
  • Face coverings except for when eating
  • 2 meter physical distancing
  • Nightclubs can operate only as restaurants & bars
  • Dancing, singing, & karaoke is permitted with restrictions
  • 2 meter physical distancing

Sports & fitness facilities

  • People in classes & spectators:
    Indoors - 50 people
    Outdoors - 100 people
  • Sports must be modified to avoid physical contact
  • Exemptions for high-performance athletes
  • Face coverings except for when exercising
  • Patron screening
  • 2 meter physical distancing

Meeting & event spaces

  • Capacity limits for venues:
    Indoors - 50 people
    Outdoors - 100 people
  • Exceptions for court and government services.
  • Patron screening

Retail

  • Passive Screening for patrons
  • Fitting rooms limited to non-adjacent stalls
  • 2 meter social distancing and face coverings required

Personal Care facilities

  • Oxygen rooms, steam rooms, and saunas are closed
  • Patron screening

Gambling establishments

  • 50 people per facility limit
  • No table games
  • Patron screening
  • 2 meter social distancing and face coverings required

Cinemas

  • Capacity limits:
    Indoors - 50 people
    Outdoors - 100 people
  • Face covering except when eating & drinking
  • Patron screening
  • Drive-in cinemas permitted to operate with restrictions

Performing arts facilities

  • Capacity limits:
    Indoors - 50 people
    Outdoors - 100 people
  • Face covering except when eating & drinking
  • Patron screening
  • Singers & players must be separated from spectators by a protective barrier
  • 2 meter social distancing except during performances
